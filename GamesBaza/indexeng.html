Valve did not keep their promise to finish working on CS2 by the end of the summer. Is this a failure or was it expected?

The release of CS2 is undeniably the main topic of 2023, capturing the full attention of the Counter-Strike community. On March 22, Valve announced the game, initiated a closed beta test, and, most importantly, set a clear release deadline for the summer of 2023. As we can see now, with September 1 on the calendar, a full release is still not available. The Igrobaza portal decided to investigate the current situation and understand how this has happened.

Assassin’s Creed Mirage - you got what you wanted. Review.

After Assassin’s Creed Syndicate, Ubisoft decided to transform the series from 'compact' action games into large-scale role-playing experiences. The size of the games grew with each release: first, a large map in Origins, then the vast landscapes of Ancient Greece in Odyssey, and this trilogy concluded with the enormous Valhalla, which took more than fifty hours to complete. Some people appreciated this approach, while others missed the old Assassin’s Creed games, where cities were significantly smaller, and navigating the map was simpler. With the release of Mirage, the desire of the old fans has been granted, and the price is set lower than the usual, at just $50 instead of the usual $70.
